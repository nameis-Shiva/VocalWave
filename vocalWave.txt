About VocalWave ------ >

1. This app allows  us to take notes about what we are speaking(it will covert the data from your voice into text).
2. It allows us the same to copy to our clipboard.
3. It will read out the text for  us.
4. It can also take the text from us and read it out for us.
5. It will "search for" us in google about anything which we ask in the form of voice, it will show the result
6. It will "go to" any website which we demand for.
7. it follows basic commands like "open youtube", "open google" etc.
